---

Notes on what this triage does:

- Vague items get deleted, not "investigated." Re-add them concretely if they're real.
- Delegate has a specific draft message, not just "ask sibling."
- Defer items have a return date *and* a location.
- "Do today" is capped at one must + three smaller items.
- The biggest hidden project (taxes) gets surfaced and offered a real plan.
- The Next 10 Minutes is a 2-minute action — opening a file, not "do the contract."
